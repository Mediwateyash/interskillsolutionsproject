What Will You Learn?

  • Data Analytics Fundamentals: Learn the key principles of data analytics, from data collection and cleaning to descriptive, diagnostic, predictive, and prescriptive analytics.

  • Machine Learning: Understand different machine learning techniques, including supervised and unsupervised learning, regression models, and decision trees.

  • Programming & Tools: Master Python, R, SQL, and other essential tools used in data analytics and AI for efficient data manipulation and analysis.
