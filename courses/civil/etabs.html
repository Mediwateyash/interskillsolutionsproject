This course is ideal for civil engineers, structural designers, and construction professionals who want to master ETABS workflows and enhance their expertise in high-rise building design. üìÖ Course Duration: 3-6 Months

ETABS is a powerful tool used in the architecture, engineering, and construction (AEC) industry for precise structural analysis, design automation, and performance evaluation.
